Rules

  • Select 2 different seeds from each region in the bracket.
  • Select one of the 8 seeds that you picked as your "superfan" school. This school will receive a 50% point bonus.

Scoring

  • Every game that your selected seeds win earns you points.
  • Points earned = Seed Number multiplied by Round Number. Example: #4 North Carolina wins in rounds 1, 2, and 3. They receive 4 x 1, 4 x 2, and 4 x 3 points for a total of 4 + 8 + 12 = 24 points.
  • The superfan school will collect 50% more points for each win. Example: Superfan pick #4 Boston College wins in rounds 1, 2, and 3. They receive 4 x 1 x 1.5, 4 x 2 x 1.5, and 4 x 3 x 1.5 points for a total of 6 + 12 + 18 = 36 points.
  • When a team loses, no more points will be scored for that selection
  • If entries are tied, the highest sum of overall seed numbers selected will win. If there is still a tie, all winnings are split
