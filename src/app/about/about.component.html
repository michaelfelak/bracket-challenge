▼ How to Play

Basic Rules

  • Select Your Teams: Choose 2 different teams from each of the 4 regions (8 teams total). You cannot select the same team twice.
  • Earn Points from Wins: Each time one of your selected teams wins in the tournament, you earn points based on that win's value.
  • Point Calculation: Points for each win = Seed Number × Round Number
    Example: A #5 seed winning in Round 2 = 5 × 2 = 10 points
  • Superfan Bonus (Optional): You may designate ONE of your 8 teams as your "superfan" team. This team earns a 50% bonus on all wins (points × 1.5).
    Example: Your superfan #5 seed winning in Round 2 = 5 × 2 × 1.5 = 15 points

Important Notes

  • Play-in Games: If you select a play-in game team, you receive points when that team advances to the main bracket (Field of 64). You do NOT receive points for winning the play-in game itself.
  • Total Possible Points: The "Total Possible Points" shown is a theoretical maximum if ALL your teams won every game. However, this is mathematically impossible since only one team from each region can advance to the next round. This number is meant as a reference point, not your likely maximum score.
  • Review Before Submitting: Carefully validate all of your picks and verify the points calculation before clicking Submit.
